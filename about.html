More About Me

Ozgur OZKAN / Cyber Security Enthusiast

This journey began with software; over time, my curiosity about cybersecurity, Linux, and system architectures drew me into this field. Despite this, I never strayed from software and continue to develop security-focused projects using C++, Python, and Bash. I prefer to produce original projects rather than follow the paths taken by the majority.

Since childhood, I have been a protective person who enjoys supporting and guiding others in areas where I have experience. When this aspect of my personality combined with my interest in cybersecurity, I realized I had found a field where I could both protect systems and contribute by advising people.

Currently, I work on SOC operations, network security, log analysis, and threat monitoring; I practice on Wazuh, OPNsense, Active Directory, and Linux. I also have a special interest in Cloud Security and aim to develop myself in this direction.

My goal is to become a cybersecurity expert who not only uses tools but also understands, analyzes, and develops the system.
